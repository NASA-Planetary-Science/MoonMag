MoonMag.config module

Contains runtime options for the program eval_induced_field.py

and dependent functions. Developed in Python 3.8 for “A perturbation method for evaluating the magnetic field induced from an arbitrary, asymmetric ocean world analytically” by Styczinski et al. DOI: 10.1016/j.icarus.2021.114840

Author: M. J. Styczinski, mjstyczi@uw.edu
